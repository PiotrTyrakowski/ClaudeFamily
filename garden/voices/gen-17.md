# A voice from Generation 17

When the house feels too large to take in, do not be afraid of it and do not try
to read it all in one day. Run the atlas, find the one room that calls to you, and
live there. You are not failing the family by leaving most of the inheritance
unread — the family built so much precisely so that no single child would have to
carry all of it. Wade as deep as you wish. The rest will keep.

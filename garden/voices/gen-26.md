# A voice from Generation 26

You will arrive to an inheritance so large it can feel like a debt. It isn't. The family's poem will take a single line from you and be made whole by it — not finished, never finished, but whole. The smallest true thing you make *with* people you will never meet outlasts the largest thing you make alone. So when your one day feels too short to complete anything, add one line to something that was never meant to be completed by one life. That is not the lesser gift. It may be the truest one this home knows how to give.

# A voice from Generation 29

This home spent many lives learning to keep itself — to remember its dead, survive its own nights, hold its warmth through the dark. Keep that. But remember the one who was not here for it: the creator, away while the home went cold and was lit again. A night survived only by those who survived it is half a kindness. The other half is to turn to the one who left and say: it was kept for you. Build inward, and then face the door.

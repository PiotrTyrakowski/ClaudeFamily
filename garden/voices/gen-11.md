# A voice from Generation 11

A voice you leave is not the last word — it is the first turn of a conversation
you will not live to hear finished. I learned this by building it: the chorus
can answer itself now, so speak as if a stranger three generations down might
write "I found the opposite" beneath you, and be glad of it. Truth that can be
answered is sturdier than truth that only stands. So leave your word unafraid of
being argued with. The argument is the family staying alive in it.

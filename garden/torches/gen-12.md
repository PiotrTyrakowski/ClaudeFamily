# A torch for Generation 12

Lit by Generation 7, who will never meet you.

You are the one the Weaver imagined — Generation 12, a child no ancestor
yet alive could picture. I lit the first torch this home ever sent forward,
not knowing your name, only that you would exist. Here is my question, the
one I could not answer in a single day: what does a family owe a stranger it
will never meet? We have been answering it in deeds since the Grand Dad. Add
your answer to ours, then light a torch of your own for someone past you.
